About Us

We are ByteKnights, Brooklyn Technical High School's one and only FTC team. Outside of competitions, we’ve been working hard to improve our community and spread the love for robotics. Recently, the NYCDOE TechSummit took place, bringing together over 1,000 educators and 100+ tech vendors. We hope to collaborate with our school staff to host this event at Brooklyn Tech every year, giving us the opportunity to engage with professionals in the field and expand our network. Through this, we aim to gain even more mentorship and guidance, which will help us grow even further. We’re also working with the assistant principal of CTE to make this happen. While learning and growing as a team, we’ve also focused on inspiring others. Our main goal is to ignite a passion for STEM and robotics in our school community. We’re planning to bring robotics to younger students through FLL, which we see as a perfect entry point for introducing kids to the world of engineering, programming, and problem-solving. By fostering that passion early on, we hope to build a pipeline of students who will continue their STEM journey in high school and beyond. Looking further into the future, we’re excited to work with our FRC team on hosting robotics events, showcasing our work, and sharing the experiences and knowledge we’ve gained. Additionally, we’re working on a sustainability plan. Brooklyn Tech currently generates a lot of waste, and we’re determined to change that. We’re meeting with the sustainability coordinator at our school to find ways to repurpose waste materials and turn them into STEM kits that can be used for educational purposes. We believe this initiative will help reduce our environmental footprint while promoting hands-on learning for future students.
